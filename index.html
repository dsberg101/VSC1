The Opportunity

Vertical video is the fastest-growing segment in global entertainment, projected to reach $8.82B by 2027. Platforms like ReelShort and DramaBox have scaled revenues by 400% – 2,600% in a single year, fueled by mobile-first audiences consuming serialized short-form stories. This is an emerging premium content category with strong global demand but a limited supply of quality producers.

Our Strategy

Our strategic investment deploys $5 million across 36 months to establish a vertically integrated content production and licensing operation, targeting proven high-growth platforms while building diversified international revenue streams.

We will begin with 2–3 pilot series in Year 1 to prove demand, secure licensing partners, and validate unit economics. From there, we scale to 12+ series per year. Our model preserves full IP ownership, enabling long-tail monetization through international sales, revenue-sharing agreements, and franchise development.
